Paleoclimate Data Visualizer

Source: PReSto Custom Reconstruction Engine (https://paleopresto.com/custom.html)

To navigate the data:

  • Use the menu bar at the top.
  • To change the year incrementally, click on the year slider and then use left and right mouse buttons.
  • Use your mouse to zoom and pan the map.
  • Click anywhere on the map to see a interactive time series at the closest available location.
  • For some data sets, click the "layers" symbol in the upper right to access regional means, then click anywhere in a region to see a regional mean.
